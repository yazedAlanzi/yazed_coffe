Espresso

Espresso coffee is a concentrated, dark Italian coffee. It consists of a little boiling water with original coffee beans and a small huna, served in a cup with a saucer. It is similar in shape to a Turkish coffee cup, but is often transparent and white in color, and can be translucent.

Espresso coffee is thicker than other coffee drinks, and has a higher concentration of suspended and dissolved materials and a creamy foam that arises from pressure, as the concentration of this drink is strongly removed in a typical cup (60 milliliters).
