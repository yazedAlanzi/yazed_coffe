Cappuccino

Interdaction of Cappuccino :

A cappuccino is a popular coffee drink made from espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam Cappuccino (Italian: Cappuccino) is a type of coffee mixed with milk. It was invented by the Italians and is sold in cafes and decorated with various types of decorations such as foam or cream. Italian coffee consisting of a small amount of espresso, to which milk and milk foam are added equally.
