Saudi Coffe

Coffee is a popular drink in the Arab world, and occupies a special place in Saudi Arabia. Saudi coffee is famous for its unique and distinctive flavour, which reflects the history and culture of the region The history of coffee in Saudi Arabia dates back to the Middle Ages, when coffee was mainly used in Andalusia and Yemen. Adopting Saudi coffee as an official drink:

In 2013, the Saudi Ministry of Commerce and Industry announced the adoption of Saudi coffee as an official drink in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. This announcement aims to promote Saudi coffee and enhance its position as a national drink.
